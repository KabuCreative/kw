Testimonials here

Independently audited and fully accredited

We are active members of Australia's peak industry bodies (AIMA and AFRA) and amongst a select group of international moving companies who have achieved FAIM accreditation for all our branches.

This accreditation gives us access to the FIDI Global Alliance, the only network of global mobility specialists who comply with an internationally recognised quality standard.

This means that when you move with us, you can be sure that you will receive the same seamless quality standard and support at over 500 destinations worldwide.

On time - guaranteed

We offer an on-time guarantee for the majority of our international shipping services - ensuring our service is one of the fastest in the market.

In the unlikely event that your belongings fail to arrive into port within the nominated time frame provided, we will refund the cost of the move - guaranteed.

Cost-effective and hassle free

We pride ourselves on commitment to quality and cost-efficiency. From your dedicated moving coordinator, to the removalists who come to your home, our staff have the training and expertise to respond to and anticipate your unique needs.
