Long-term or temporary storage

To help make your move hassle-free we offer long-term or temporary storage in both our Australian warehouses and international depots.

Storage can be arranged at either end of your move, or at a third location, depending on your requirements.

Contact us or request a quote for more information.

Secure

Our storage facilities are secured by CCTV, monitored alarm systems and patrolled security.

Clean

We regularly clean and fumigate our storage facilities to ensure a pest-free environment.

Cost-effective

Storing your goods with us is significantly more cost-effective than choosing a self-storage solution.

Frequently asked questions

I only want to store a few items. Can you still help me?

Yes we can. We have a range of options suitable for storing goods of different sizes. Please contact us to discuss your requirements.

How often can I access my stored goods?

This depends on where your goods are stored. For storage at our Sydney, Melbourne and Canberra warehouses, you can access your goods during standard business hours.

How do your prices compare to self-storage solutions?

Storing your goods in our Australian warehouses is significantly more cost-effective than choosing a 24-hour access self-storage solution.

Where are your storage facilities located?

We can arrange for your possessions to be stored in almost any location worldwide. Our primary local facilities are:

  • Melbourne: Mount Waverley
  • Sydney: Ermington
  • Canberra: Hume
