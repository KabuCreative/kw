Moving your goods - faster

It is not uncommon for shipping companies to hold your goods in their warehouse until they are able to fill an entire container for your destination. This means that customers who are not shipping a full container of possessions can wait weeks, or even months, for their goods to depart Australia. This is particularly common for consignments to less popular destinations.

At King & Wilson we understand that you want to receive your possessions as soon as possible and are committed to the high-frequency departure of all our containers - whether they are full or not.

For your peace of mind, we offer an on-time guarantee - if your goods do not arrive into their destination port on time, we'll refund your shipping costs.

The transit time is defined as commencing from the date when payment and all completed shipping documents have been received and processed for shipping and the consignment arrives into one our warehouses in either Sydney, Melbourne or Canberra. The transit time then finishes on the date the shipment arrives into the destination port or airport. The guarantee excludes time taken for our destination agent to customs clear and arrange delivery to residence, shipment delays which are a result of events outside our control such as strikes, wars and natural disasters or delays by the border authorities of the destination country such as Customs, Homeland Security or Environmental Agencies.
