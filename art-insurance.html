Cost-effective art transport insurance

Whilst it is unusual for items to be lost or damanged in transit, for your peace of mind we recommend you take out transit insurance for the period your objects are being moved and stored.

To that end, we have developed an insurance policy specifically designed to protect your fine arts and antiques during transit and/or storage in our warehouses. We provide an Indemnity Cover policy for works of art and antiques.

For more insurance information speak to your moving consultant, or download our documentation booklet which contains our financial services guide, product disclosure statement, and insurance policy wording.

Flexible

We can customise insurance policies to suit your budget and the level of risk involved in transporting your artwork. We can offer a fully comprehensive policy or simply cover you for loss. Our premiums are calculated according to the declared value of the object.

Government accredited

We are amongst a select group of removalist companies accredited to arrange insurance for our customers as per the Commonwealth Financial Services Act.

Value for money

Our policies are designed to offer you quality coverage at a competitive price.
