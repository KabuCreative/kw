Ship a small amount by sea or air

Moving a small amount from Canberra? Our international baggage service is perfect for the safe transportation of personal quantities of clothing, books, sporting equipment and household belongings.

Depending on the amount you wish to move, along with your final destination, we can arrange for your belongings to be transported by sea or air.

Our excess baggage service includes:

  • Providing you with appropriate export quality boxes to pack your possessions.
  • Collecting your belongings from your Canberra home, or receiving them at our warehouse.
  • Security clearing your belongings (for possessions moved by air).
  • Weighing and measuring your belongings.
  • Ensuring your belongings clear customs at both ends of their journey.
  • Covering your terminal charges.
  • Delivering your belongings at your new residence.

Frequently asked questions

How much does it cost?

This depends on the amount you wish to ship and the final destination. Please contact us or request an instant quote for more information.

Is there anything I can't send?

Yes, there are a number of belongings that are prohibited, including LPG gas bottles, paints, aerosols and solvents.

A full list of prohibited items will be provided to you by your moving consultant.

How is the cost calculated?

For shipments sent by sea the cost is calculated by volume (in square meters).

To make matters more complicated, the formula for chargeable weight changes depending on the airline or air courier we partner with to ship your belongings.

Do I need insurance?

Whilst it is unusual for items to be damaged or lost in transit, for your peace of mind we recommend that you take out transit insurance. To help you get the right cover, we have put together a cost-effective policy specifically designed to protect your belongings during transit and/or storage in our warehouses.
