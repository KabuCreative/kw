faq: > Can I pack my boxes myself?

You can pack your own boxes, however airline (and airport) security specifies that all boxes must be packed by our accredited (and security certified) packers.

This means our staff are required to open any customer packed boxes and check the contents before re-sealing them with security tape.

Is there anything I can't send?

Yes, there are a number of belongings that are prohibited by airlines and airports, including LPG gas bottles, paints, aerosols and solvents.

A full list of prohibited items will be provided to you by your moving consultant.

How much will it cost?

This depends on the amount you are shipping and the final destination.

In some cases our air-freight service is more cost-effective than shipping by sea; this is particularly common when your destination is some distance from a major international sea port.

It is also not uncommon for smaller smaller shipments of personal effects (up to three boxes) to be cheaper to move by air.

Request a Quote or contact us for an accurate estimate.

To make matters more complicated, the formula for chargeable weight changes depending on the airline or air courier we partner with to ship your belongings.

In all cases, we move your belongings in the most cost-effective manner so to pass on the best possible price to you. Contact us to receive an accurate, no obligation quote.

Do I need insurance?

Whilst it is unusual for items to be damaged or lost in transit, for your peace of mind we recommend that you take out transit insurance. To help you get the right cover, we have put together a cost-effective policy specifically designed to protect your belongings during transit and/or storage.

Request a Quote or contact us to speak to a moving consultant about the appropriate insurance for your move.

International air freight

Air freight is a fast and cost-effective solution for moving critical items such as clothing, files, tools, office equipment and children's toys. Many of our customers choose to use air freight in conjunction with our international sea freight service.

Our security certified air freight service includes:

  • We conduct an in-home consultation to guide you through the moving process, including:
    • Evaluating any specialist packing requirements
    • Recommending an air package size
    • Determining the best shipping schedule to suit your needs
    • Explaining transit insurance policies
    This process usually takes between 30 minutes and one hour.
  • Packing your belongings in accordance with international air freight standards.
  • Weighing and measuring your belongings.
  • Ensuring your belongings clear customs at both ends of their journey.
  • Covering your terminal charges.
  • Delivering and unpacking your belongings at your new residence and disposing of any mess.
