International Air Freight

Air freight is a fast and cost-effective solution for moving critical items such as clothing, files, tools, office equipment and children's toys. Many of our customers choose to use air freight in conjunction with our international sea freight service.

Our security certified air freight service includes:

  • In home consultation to guide you through the moving process, recommend an air package size and determine the best schedule for your needs.
  • Packing your goods in accordance with international air freight standards.
  • Weighing and measuring your goods.
  • Ensuring your goods clear customs at both ends of their journey.
  • Covering your terminal charges.
  • Delivering and unpacking your goods at your new residence and disposing of any mess.

Frequently Asked Questions:

Can I pack my boxes myself?

No, you cannot. Airline (and airport) security specifies that all boxes must be packed by our accredited (and security certified) packers.

Is there anything I can't send?

Yes, there are a number of goods that are prohibited by airlines and airports, including LPG gas bottles, paints, aerosols and solvents.

A full list of prohibited items will be provided to you by your moving consultant.

How much will it cost?

This depends on the amount you are shipping and the final destination.

Whilst more expensive than sea freight, shipping by air is approximately TODO times more cost-effective than paying airline excess baggage fees.

Request a quote or contact us for an accurate estimate.

Do I need insurance?

Whilst it is unusual for items to be damaged or lost in transit, for your peace of mind we recommend that you take out transit insurance. To help you get the right cover, we have put together a cost-effective policy specifically designed to protect your goods during transit and/or storage in our warehouses.

Request a quote or contact us to speak to a moving consultant about the appropriate insurance for your move.
