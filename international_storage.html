Long-term or Temporary Storage at Major International Locations

Many of our clients who are moving internationally require short or long-term storage at their destination.

As well as offering storage in our Australian warehouses, we can assist in organising the storage of your goods with our international partners.

We only partner with world-leading international removalists, so you can be sure your possessions will be held in clean, secure facilties.

Contact us or request a quote for more information on our international storage service.
