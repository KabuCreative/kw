faq: > I only want to store a few items. Can you still help me?

Yes we can. We can arrange a suitable option for storing belongings of different sizes. Please contact us to discuss your requirements.

How often can I access my stored belongings?

This depends on where your belongings are stored. In many storage facilities you can access your belongings during standard business hours, under supervision.

How do your prices compare to self-storage solutions?

Storing your belongings with our international partners is generally more cost-effective than choosing a 24-hour access self-storage solution.

Are there anything I can't store?

Flammable or toxic items such as fuel, gases, paints, poisons, aerosol sprays are not permitted, nor are perishable items such as food and plants.

Do you have the facilities for storing large/unique items?

Yes we can safely and securely store large, unique or valuable items anywhere in the world.

Do I need insurance?

Yes. We can arrange for specialist storage insurance for possessions stored overseas. Please contact us for more information.
