Fine art logistics and storage

King & Wilson operate a specialist fine art moving team - King & Wilson Essential Art Services.

This division is equipped with specialist fine art moving facilites and staffed by an expert team, many of whom are trained fine art practitioners.

Each year our Essential Art Services team move hundreds of paintings, sculptures and installations for leading galleries and organisations.

Our services include:

  • Moving fine art and installations locally, interstate or internationally
  • Storing fine art locally and internationally in secure, climate controlled facilities
  • Providing end-to-end logistical support for fairs and exhibitions

Visit the King & Wilson Essential Art Services website for more information.
