Moving your art interstate - with absolute care

With our dedicated interstate art transport service, King & Wilson Essential Art Services can offer competitively priced interstate solutions for moving your precious art and antiques. Interstate journeys are coordinated and performed by our specialist Fine Art transport team, purpose-built fleet and professional craters.

Our secure vehicles are custom built for fine art transport and feature racking and carton hold areas specifically designed to prevent movement and vibration. Insulated and equipped with GPS tracking and tail-lifts, our fleet is only operated by trained members of the King & Wilson team.

Melbourne - Sydney art shuttle service

We run a dedicated weekly fine art shuttle service between Melbourne and Sydney utilising our air ride climate controlled (temperature and humidity to museum specifications) art truck.

This service departs Melbourne each Tuesday, which means Sydney deliveries can be made Wednesday, Thursday and Fridays. The return service departs Sydney each Wednesday via Canberra with deliveries in Melbourne on Friday, Monday or Tuesday.

We also offer regular interstate art transport services to destinations such as Perth, Brisbane, Adelaide, Hobart, Cairns, Townsville, Darwin and Alice Springs.

Expert handling and processes

We have transported a wide range of art objects, large and small, for private collectors as well as museums, galleries and auction houses. Our fine art transport team has the expertise to relocate entire exhibitions, including packing and crating, transport and installation.

Secure equipment and facilities

Our secure vehicles are custom built for art transport and feature racking and carton hold areas specifically designed to prevent movement and vibration. Insulated and equipped with GPS tracking and tail-lifts, our fleet is only operated by trained members of our fine art services team.

Professional art packing

Our experienced packing and crating team use a range of industry preferred materials and standardised techniques to prepare your art objects of any size, weight, sensitivity and number for any mode of transport. Our exhibition crates are made to be easily unpacked and re-packed.

Our specialist art packing materials include:

  • Export approved timber ISPM15 Certification
  • Cardboard cases (Art packs to suit many sizes of canvas)
  • Tyvek
  • Softlon
  • Cellaire wrap
  • Ethafoam

Our manual handling processes and procedures have been designed in consultation with professional conservators to ensure outstanding quality and consistency in all our packing tasks.

Should you require it, we can also arrange:

  • Short or long term fine art storage in our secure facilities
