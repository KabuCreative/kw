Custom crating - casing for international transport, art fairs and museum travelling exhibitions

King & Wilson Essential Art Services has developed a reputation for manufacturing high quality bespoke crates.

Our experienced team is able to design and fabricate a range of crates including:

  • Multi-use museum travel frames and crates
  • Built in frameworks
  • Gasket seals
  • Slotting
  • Supports and cushioning
  • Single use destination-only crates (a more economical option)

Should you require it, we can also manufacture bespoke tri-wall cardboard boxes, cylinders and slat crates.

Contact us to discuss your crating requirements.
