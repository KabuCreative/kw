Transit insurance for your peace of mind

Whilst most of our clients hold contents insurance of one kind or another, many are unaware that these policies often fail to cover the transit of goods from one home to another.

Whilst it is unusual for items to be lost or damanged in transit, for your peace of mind we recommend you take out moving insurance for the period your prossessions are being moved and stored.

To help you get the right cover, we have put together a cost-effective policy specifically designed to protect your goods during transit and/or storage in our warehouses.

At a glance

When you take a policy with us, you will be covered for:

  • Breakage
  • Scratching
  • Chipping
  • Denting
  • Theft and Pilferage
  • Non-delivery

Specified Value Insurance

  • You nominate the value of your goods (with the assistance of our inventory)
  • If the goods are lost or totally destroyed, you receive the declared value of those goods
  • If the goods are damaged and can be repaired, this policy will cover the reasonable cost of repair or restoration up to the declared value

Easy Valuation Insurance

  • Insures your total goods at replacement value - using a minimum value of A$2800 per cubic metre
  • If the goods are lost or totally destroyed, you receive A$2800 per cubic meter
  • If the goods are damaged and can be repaired, this policy will cover the reasonable cost of repair or restoration up to $2800 per cubic meter

Additional cover is available to cover the risks associated with:

  • Items that form part of a pair or set of goods
  • Mechanical, electrical or electronic derangement

Frequently asked questions

I am only moving a few items, do I need insurance?

Even if you are only moving a few boxes you should still consider taking out insurance.

Can I be insured if I pack my own boxes?

Because your goods have not been packed by a removalist, our policy excludes the contents of these cartons in the event of any damages/breakages.

Owner packed cartons can only be insured for Restricted Cover, which provides cover for the following risks only:

  • Fire and explosion
  • Lightning, flood
  • Overturning and/or derailment of conveyance
  • Collision of vessel, aircraft or conveyance
  • Crashing or forced landing of aircraft; stranding, sinking, or contact of vessel with any external object other than water
  • Entry of water into any vessel, hold, container liftvan or place of storage

Non-delivery of owner packed cartons is limited to $500.00 unless an itemised valued list of contents is supplied prior to the commencement of the transit.

Should I take insurance while my possessions are in storage?

Yes. We can arrange an extension of your transit insurance to cover your goods whilst they are in storage with us. The storage extension cover is available anywhere in Australia or for storage at any of our nominated agents worldwide.

I'm not sure I should bother. Shouldn't my home and contents insurance cover it? Or your insurance?

Ultimately, it is your decision.

There are many circumstances for which we, as your remover, may not be liable to make good any loss or damage to your goods - this is why we strongly recommend you take out transit insurance.

If you think you don't need insurance, you should ask yourself why you would insure your goods in your house (where there is low risk), and not when they are moving into a high risk situation.

You should certainly check with your home and contents insurer to find out if your existing policy covers the transportation of your possessions.
