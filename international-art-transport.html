Moving your art overseas - with absolute care

Our fine art transport team can coordinate your complete international art transport solution. Our services include local pick-up by art handlers, preparation, crating, customs advice, carnets and documentation, air terminal supervision, overseas delivery and insurance.

King & Wilson Essential Art Services has affiliations with an extensive network of fine art agents around the world, ensuring that we are able to professionally handle works of any scale and complexity both into and out of Australia.

We have the resources and experience to create a customised solution to suit your budget, time constraints and the particular requirements for the safety and security of your art objects.

Expert handling and processes

We have transported a wide range of art objects, large and small, for private collectors as well as museums, galleries and auction houses. Our fine art transport team has the expertise to relocate entire exhibitions, including packing and crating, transport and installation.

Secure equipment and facilities

Our secure vehicles are custom built for art transport and feature racking and carton hold areas specifically designed to prevent movement and vibration. Insulated and equipped with GPS tracking and tail-lifts, our fleet is only operated by trained members of our fine art services team.

Professional art packing

Our experienced packing and crating team use a range of industry preferred materials and standardised techniques to prepare your art objects of any size, weight, sensitivity and number for any mode of transport. Our exhibition crates are made to be easily unpacked and re-packed.

Our specialist art packing materials include:

  • Export approved timber ISPM15 Certification
  • Cardboard cases (Art packs to suit many sizes of canvas)
  • Tyvek
  • Softlon
  • Cellaire wrap
  • Ethafoam

Our manual handling processes and procedures have been designed in consultation with professional conservators to ensure outstanding quality and consistency in all our packing tasks.

Should you require it, we can also arrange:

  • Short or long term fine art storage in our secure facilities
  • Cost-effective art transport insurance

Do you move my art by sea or air?

We can move your art by either service, depending on your budget and requirements. Request a quote for more information.

Will I have to pay customs, duties and taxes when sending artwork overseas?

This depends on the country you are shipping to and the purpose for the shipment. We will direct you to the relevant government services for your enquiries.

Can you handle large, awkward, heavy objects such as large bronze sculptures and installations consisting of many objects?

Yes, we have access to cranes, forklifts, slings and hydraulic tail lift equipment to safely transport your large, sensitive, awkward or heavy objects.

Can you guarantee specific climate conditions during transport?

By choosing the right packing method, we can insulate your artwork from changes in temperature during transit. We have a range of insulating materials for packing and crating.
