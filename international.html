International moving specialists by air or by sea.

King & Wilson have the processes, expertise and international network to make your overseas removal as streamlined as possible.

From packing, storage, customs processes or transit insurance, we can assist you every step of the way.

Our processes and services are benchmarked to world standards. Here's how it works:

  • We conduct an in-home consultation to guide you through the moving process, recommend a container size or air freight option and determine the best shipping schedule for you. This process usually takes between 30 minutes and one hour
  • On the scheduled day, we visit your home to pack your cartons and wrap your furniture to export standards
  • Your goods are transferred to our warehouse and then onto the appropriate plane or ship
  • We ensure all of your neccessary paperwork is in order to faciliate prompt customs clearance
  • We deliver the goods to your new home - unpacking the cartons, placing furniture and disposing of packing debris

We pride ourselves on providing you with as much assistance as you need to make your move stress free. Should you require it, we can arrange:

  • Storage of your goods at either end of your move.
  • Cleaning of your home
  • Visas and foreign exchange

Frequently Asked Questions:

I only want to send a few items, can you still help me?

Yes we can. Our excess baggage department specialises in packing and shipping small quantities of unaccompanied personal effects by sea or air.

To get an instant quote, please visit link

Can I pack my own boxes?

Yes, you can pack your own boxes. Some clients elect to pack clothes, books and linen and leave the more fragile items to us. If you choose to do this, we will supply you with appropriate export quality boxes and packing materials.

We can also provide you with instructions for packing your boxes to export standards, however we can not be responsible for any breakages in boxes packed by you.

Cartons and belongings which are to be transported overseas by air are required to be packed and sealed by King & Wilson security cleared staff.

Do I need insurance?

Whilst it is unusual for items to be damaged or lost in transit, for your peace of mind we recommend that you take out transit insurance. To help you get the right cover, we have put together a cost-effective policy specifically designed to protect your goods during transit and/or storage in our warehouses.

Request a quote or contact us to speak to a moving consultant about the appropriate insurance for your move.

How long will it take for my goods to arrive?

This depends on whether you are sending your items by air or sea as well as the final destination.

Air transit times can be found on the air freight page.

Transit times for sea shipments can be found on the individual destination pages (linked to above).

How much notice do I need to provide?

The amount of notice required depends on the nature of your move. We recommend that you book your international removal three to four weeks prior to departure. You should allow one to two weeks notice for an Interstate removal. These suggested lead times will allow us to secure and confirm your booking for your preferred dates and give you sufficient time to prepare for your move. Our Moving Planners contain a useful time-line schedule of helpful tips to guide you through the process and have you ready for moving day.

Please note that a greater leader time is required for moves planned for the peak Summer period which runs from December to January.

Do I need to be there on the moving day?

No, you do not need to be present for the entire packing and moving process. However, if you are unable to attend in person, it is important that you nominate an authorised representative to allow our crews access to you home and be present to observe and sign the compiliation of your packing inventory for all interstate, storage and international moves.
