Relocation services

We regularly arrange relocation services for our corporate clients and are pleased to extend these services to you.

Should you require it, we can arrange:

  • Orientation and settling-in services
  • Temporary accommodation, rental home search and home purchasing assistance
  • School/education search
  • Departure services

Household services

Cleaning

We can arrange a full house clean, including window, oven and carpet cleaning. This can be timed to commence as your last items are being moved out.

Valet unpacking

We provide trained and professional valet assistants to unpack your cartons, clean your belongings and place them into freshly wiped cupboards.

Beds are made up with fresh linen, books arranged onto bookshelves and crystal and silverware sensibly sorted into buffets and wall units.

Handyman assist

In the process of moving, there may be jobs which require the assistance of a carpenter or handyman. This could include dismantling and reassembling furniture such as wardrobes and wall units or hanging pictures and mirrors.

If you are not the handy type, King & Wilson can provide qualified personnel to carry out these tasks and help make your move as smooth as possible.

Specialist moving services

If you can imagine it, we've moved it! We pride ourselves on our flexibility to accommodate your specific moving requirements and can assist with:

  • Plant transportation by a specialist aborist
  • Pet transportation and boarding
  • Vehicle tranportation
  • Antique and fine art transportation

Contact us or speak to your moving consultant for more information on any of the services listed above.
