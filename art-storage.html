Secure, climate controlled art storage

We offer long and short-term climate controlled fine art storage in our purpose built art storage facilities in Sydney and Melbourne. Our climate controlled art storage facilities feature:

  • 24 hour alarm (externally monitored and patrolled)
  • Monitored fire protection
  • Recorded CCTV surveillance
  • Restricted and logged door access
  • Humidity control (relative humidity 55% ± 2%)
  • Climate control at (21°C ± 1°C)
  • Painting racks
  • Crate Storage
  • Electronic inventory consignment control

All artwork in our care is periodically inspected by our experienced art handlers and client access is only permitted under King & Wilson staff supervision.
