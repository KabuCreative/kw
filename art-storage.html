Secure, climate controlled art storage

We offer long term storage and short term climate controlled fine art storage in our purpose built art storage facilities in Sydney and Melbourne. Our climate controlled art storage facilities feature:

  • 24 hour alarm (externally monitored and patrolled)
  • Monitored fire protection
  • Recorded CCTV surveillance
  • Restricted and logged door access
  • Humidity control (55% ± 2%)
  • Climate control at (20°C ± 1°C)
  • Painting racks
  • Crate Storage
  • Electronic inventory

All artwork in our care is periodically inspected by our experienced art handlers.
