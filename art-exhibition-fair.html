Exhibition and fair services

King & Wilson Essential Art Services works with museums, galleries and art institutions to coordinate packing and transport services for exhibitions and fairs, domestically and internationally.

Experienced

We understand the demands placed on curators, registrars and gallerists. King & Wilson Essential Art Services can assist and support you in navigating complex customs procedures and logistical challenges throughout the entire project. With many years of experience behind us, our art team is critically engaged with Australian and international art practice and events.

Specialists

King & Wilson Essential Art Services specialises in the analysis, evaluation, and organisation of major logistical movements, including collection relocations, travelling exhibitions and major art fairs. We have experience in working under some of the most difficult and challenging conditions and locations.

Trusted

King & Wilson Essential Art Services are the trusted partner of Sydney Contemporary and the Melbourne International Art Fair.
