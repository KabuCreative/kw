Exhibition and fair services

King & Wilson Essential Art Services works with museums, galleries and art institutions to coordinate packing and transport services for exhibitions and fairs, domestically and internationally.

Experienced

We understand the demands placed on curators. Our company can assist and support you in navigating complex customs procedures and logistical challenges throughout the transport of your precious objects. With many years of experience behind us, our fine art team share an active interest in visual arts programmes.

Specialist

King & Wilson Essential Art Services specialise in the analysis, evaluation, and organisation of major logistical movements, including collection relocations, household moves, and architectural project management. We have experience in working under some of the most difficult and challenging conditions and locations.

Trusted

We are trusted partners of many of the world's largest art fairs, including:

  • Hong Kong Art Fair
  • Armory Art Fair
  • Dubai Art Fair
  • Tokyo Art Fair
  • Art Stage Singapore
  • Taipei Art Fair
  • Brussels Art Fair
  • Art Basel
  • Auckland Art Fair
  • Melbourne Art Fair
