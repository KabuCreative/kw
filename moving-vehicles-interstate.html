faq: > How will my car be transported?

Vehicles are collected from the your residence or workplace using a single car transporter, they are then consolidated for interstate transportation using either a ten car double deck curtain side road transporter or else fully enclosed custom rail containers.

Can I move caravans, boats, trailers or other large vehicles?

Yes upon request. We can also arrange for secure interstate motorcycle transportation.

How much will it cost?

This depends on the size of the vehicle and the move distance. Indicative rates for major routes are:

  • Sydney to Melbourne - approx $600
  • Melbourne to Sydney - approx $720
  • Canberra to Melbourne - approx $530
  • Sydney to Perth - approx $1510
  • Melbourne to Perth - approx $1560
  • Sydney to Brisbane - approx $730
  • Melbourne to Brisbane - approx $1080

To request a more accurate quote, or for routes not listed below, please request a quote.

Are there any extra costs I should be aware of?

Light commercial vehicles and 4WDs have higher charges than standard sedans, station wagons and hatchbacks.

Interstate car shipping

If you are moving interstate, King & Wilson can arrange safe and effective transportation of your car, 4WD or motorcycle.

Our relationship with national car transportation suppliers ensures we can coordinate local pickup of the vehicle from your home or workplace right through to your nominated destination address.

All vehicles under $100,000 are insured against market valuations, whilst a premium VIP service is offered for vehicles with valuations exceeding this amount.
