Independently audited for excellence

We are amongst a select group of removalist companies to hold FAIM quality assurance, awarded by FIDI, the most prestigious moving association in the world.

Our FAIM accreditation is independently audited every three years by Ernst & Young and benchmarked by the FAIM Coordination Centre in Brussels. This provides our customers with the peace of mind they are receiving the highest quality consistent moving standards across the world.
